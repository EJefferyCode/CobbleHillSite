+ Who We Are

We are the Cobble Hill Commission, located at 12 Smith Street in Cobble Hill, Brooklyn! We are a friendly community of local residents and Cobble Hill enthusiasts, dedicated to preserving the culture of the community, and the development of local businesses.

+ What We've Done

Over the past 20 years, the Cobble Hill Commission has created many opportunities for the community of Cobble Hill, Brooklyn to increase tourism, as well as develop local businesses, as well as opportunities for families and individuals alike to enjoy the neighbourhood.
